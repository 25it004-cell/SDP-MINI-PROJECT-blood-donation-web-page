Why Donate Blood?

Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood. Your small act of kindness can help someone fight for their life.

Who Can Donate?

  • Age between 18 to 65 years
  • Minimum weight 50 kg
  • Healthy and free from infections
  • Should have slept at least 6 hours
